## Key Insights
- Indian EdTech market projected to grow significantly through 2033.
- Tech upskilling emerged as the highest-growth segment driven by demand for AI, Data Science, Cloud, and Generative AI skills.
- Learners prioritize career outcomes, salary growth, certifications, and placement support.
- Outcome-based learning models and AI-personalized learning are becoming major differentiators.
