# Google Pay – SpendWise AI

## Problem Statement
Google Pay provides transaction history and spending data, but users struggle to understand the behavioral reasons behind their spending, leading to confusion, guilt, and disengagement from financial insights.

## Objective
Help users move from transaction tracking to spending understanding by providing contextual, non-judgmental explanations behind their spending behavior.

## User Pain Points
- Users know what they spent but not why they spent it.
- Transaction lists and charts are difficult to interpret.
- Spending reflection often feels judgmental and emotionally uncomfortable.
- Users avoid reviewing spending due to guilt or anxiety.
- Existing insights lack behavioral and contextual explanations.

## Proposed Solutions
- AI-generated spending insights using transaction patterns and contextual signals.
- User-correctable explanations that allow users to confirm, dismiss, or edit AI interpretations.
- Reflection prompts triggered after meaningful spending events.
- Context-aware spending narratives instead of raw transaction summaries.
- Personalized behavioral pattern detection to improve self-awareness.

## Success Metrics
- Verified Context Ratio (% of AI insights confirmed or reframed by users)
- Insight Revisit Rate
- Edit vs Dismiss Ratio
- Post-Insight App Exit Rate
- Insight Engagement Rate
- Repeat Insight Usage
