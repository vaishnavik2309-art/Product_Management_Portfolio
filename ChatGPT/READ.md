# ChatGPT Voice Input Adoption for Indian Students

## Overview
Led an end-to-end product thinking exercise to increase voice input adoption on ChatGPT Mobile among Indian students. Conducted market research, user interviews, competitive analysis, solution prioritization, and PRD creation to identify adoption barriers and design a scalable solution.

## Problem
Despite widespread adoption of voice technology in India, students primarily rely on typing while using ChatGPT. Key barriers included low feature discoverability, lack of trust in transcription accuracy, privacy concerns, and established typing habits.

## Research Insights
- Conducted qualitative and quantitative user research
- Analyzed voice usage trends in India
- Benchmarked ChatGPT against WhatsApp, Google Assistant, and Alexa
- Identified awareness, accuracy, and social discomfort as primary adoption barriers

## Solution
Designed a **VoiceBot Popup with Dynamic Transcription**, a lightweight onboarding experience that:
- Improves voice feature discoverability
- Builds trust through real-time transcription feedback
- Encourages first-time voice usage
- Supports editing before submission to reduce user anxiety

## Product Strategy
- Evaluated multiple solutions using the RICE framework
- Defined North Star Metric: Voice Adoption Rate
- Created user journeys, wireframes, and onboarding flows
- Designed telemetry and event tracking requirements
- Planned rollout strategy, A/B testing, and success measurement framework
