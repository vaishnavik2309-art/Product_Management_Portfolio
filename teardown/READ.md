# n8n Onboarding Teardown

## Overview
Analyzed n8n's onboarding journey to understand how different types of users experience the product and identify areas causing drop-offs during activation.

## What I Did
- Studied n8n's product positioning and target users
- Analyzed onboarding journeys for technical and non-technical users
- Identified friction points in the activation funnel
- Evaluated onboarding experience from a user perspective
- Proposed improvements to increase workflow creation and activation

## Key Observations
- Technical users quickly understand the product's value and create workflows successfully.
- Non-technical users often struggle with technical terminology, blank screens, and credential setup.
- Many users drop off before running their first workflow.
- The onboarding experience favors experienced users over beginners.

## Recommendations
- Add guided onboarding flows
- Provide beginner-friendly workflow templates
- Simplify credential setup
- Introduce onboarding checklists
- Personalize onboarding based on user goals

## Outcome
Created a set of onboarding recommendations aimed at reducing friction and helping more users successfully create their first workflow.
